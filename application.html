Buying Process
We do X-Rays on the mother between days 50-55 to get a good idea count of how many puppies we can expect. After we can get an idea of how many puppies we can expect, we ask for a small REFUNDABLE deposit of $50.00. Deposits are taken on a first come first serve basis and in order of payment not promise to pay on this date. After all the puppies are born and everyone is cleaned up, fed and taken care of we will take some new born pictures and post online and send them to those who placed a deposit.

After week 2 we go in order of depsoit to allow the picks to be chosen. By this time, you'll be able to see the coat and we can tell you about the puppies personalities. When you confirm your pick, a non-refundable deposit of $250.00 will ne necessary to lock in your pick (if you already paid $50.00 your deposit will be $200.00).

Pictures and videos will be posted each week on social media and sent to you. You'll also receive weekly updates on your puppy. Your puppy will receive deworming medicine at weeks 2,4,6 and 8. It will be vet checked at Cumberland Valley Animal Hospital. There it will given its first round of puppy shots and cleared before going to it's forever home. Once the vet gives the puppy his final stamp of approval, we will send the documents from the Vet and the remaining payment will be due within 3 days and before the puppy is picked up.

The cost per puppy will range between $900.00-$1,200.00 and depsoits will be subtracted from this total.
