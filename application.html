Buying Process
We do X-Rays on the mother between days 50-55 to get a good idea count of how many puppies we can expect. After we can get an idea of how many puppies we can expect, we ask for a small REFUNDABLE deposit of $50.00. Deposits are taken on a first come first serve basis and in order of payment not promise to pay on this date. You'll simply visit the CURRENT PUPS page and choose your color. After all the puppies are born and everyone is cleaned up, fed and taken care of we will take some new born pictures and post online and send them to those who placed a deposit.

After week 2 we go in order of deposit to allow the picks to be chosen. By this time, you'll be able to see the coat and we can tell you about the puppies personalities. When you confirm your pick, a deposit of $250.00 will ne necessary to lock in your pick (if you already paid $50.00 your deposit will be $200.00).

Pictures and videos will be posted each week on social media and sent to you. You'll also receive weekly updates on your puppy. Your puppy will receive de-worming medicine at 3 weeks and 6 weeks. At 6 weeks they will also get their first full round of shots. It will be vet checked at Cumberland Valley Animal Hospital and cleared before going to it's forever home. Once the vet gives the puppy his final stamp of approval, the remaining balance will be due before the puppy is picked up and you'll receive all vet documents at that time.

The cost per puppy will range between $900.00-$1,200.00 and deposits will be subtracted from this total.
