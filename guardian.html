Guardian Home Process

A guardian home gets the best of the best puppy from a litter that we want to place into our breeding program. We consider things such as color, coat, temperament and obedience while they're with us. A Guardian Home isn't a free puppy. It's a commitment to raise and care for a breeder's dog as a loved family pet for the duration of its breeding career. If you are interested in becoming a Guardian Home family, please fill out the following application and we'll be in contact. Guardian Home Application
