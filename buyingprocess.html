Buying Process

You can check out our Coming Soon page for when we expect upcoming litters. We will announce on our Facebook Page when we will start taking deposits on those litters. When litters are confirmed, we ask for a non-refundable $50 deposit via Stripe or Venmo to reserve your spot in line.

Our goal is to make the process simple and transparent from the very beginning. Below is an overview of what to expect from the time you reach out to us until your puppy goes home at around eight weeks of age.

1

Connect & Reserve Your Spot

Start by contacting us about an upcoming or current litter. Once we’ve talked through timing and fit for your family, you can place your $50 non-refundable deposit via Stripe or Venmo to reserve a spot for that litter. If timing changes, your deposit can be moved to a future litter that works better for your schedule.

Communication: After your deposit is placed, we’ll confirm your spot, review expected timelines, and share how updates and selection will work for your specific litter.

2

Birth & Early Updates

Once puppies arrive, we share photos from birth and again once eyes open around 2–3 weeks of age. Pictures and videos are posted on social media and on our website, and we can also send them directly to families on the reservation list.

We aim to update families several times as puppies grow so you can watch your future puppy develop from the very beginning.

3

Health Care & Growing Weeks

Puppies receive de-worming medicine at 3 weeks and again at 6 weeks of age. Around 5–6 weeks, we share another round of photos or videos so you can see coats and personalities starting to shine.

At 6 weeks they will also get their first full round of shots. Each puppy is vet checked and cleared before going to its forever home, and you’ll receive a complete veterinary health record at pickup.

4

Puppy Selection

After the 6-week vet check, families can schedule an in-person selection visit. For those who live farther away or prefer not to travel, we can also assist with selection by FaceTime/video on request. We’ll talk through your preferences for gender, temperament, coat type, and markings to help match you with the right puppy.

5

Preparing for Go-Home Day

Puppies are typically ready to go home around 8 weeks of age. Once the vet gives the puppy a final stamp of approval, the remaining balance will be due before or at the time the puppy is picked up.

We prefer cash at pickup, but we can also send a secure Stripe link ahead of time or accept Venmo at pickup (Friends & Family only).

6

Go-Home Day & Health Guarantee

On pickup day you’ll receive all vet documents, our one-year genetic health guarantee, and a small goody bag to help your puppy settle into their new home.

Families are expected to keep up with their puppy’s vaccines and ongoing health care with their own veterinarian, which is required for the genetic health guarantee to remain in effect.
