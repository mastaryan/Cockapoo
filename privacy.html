Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Bluegrass Doodles, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact forms — name, email, phone number, and message details.
  • Puppy reservation deposits — processed securely through Stripe or Venmo.
  • Website analytics — anonymous usage data via Google Analytics 4 (GA4).
  • Affiliate links — Amazon and Chewy may track clicks for purchase attribution.
  • Cookies & tracking technologies — used to improve user experience and website performance.

How Your Information Is Used

We use collected data to:

  • Respond to inquiries and contact form submissions
  • Process and confirm puppy reservations
  • Improve website experience and troubleshoot issues
  • Track marketing performance and website traffic
  • Provide product recommendations through affiliate partnerships

Payment Privacy

All payments are processed securely through Stripe or Venmo. We do not store or have access to your full credit card details.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some product links on our website are affiliate links through Amazon Associates and Chewy. We may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you.

Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

We use GA4 and Google Signals to understand how visitors use our site. This includes anonymous aggregated data such as:

  • Page views and interactions
  • Device type and general location
  • Traffic sources and user behavior

GA4 does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, or address.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Data may be shared only with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, Venmo)
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics)
  • Marketing platforms (e.g., Meta for ad performance)

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your information when legally permitted
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics, performance, and optional personalized content. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
