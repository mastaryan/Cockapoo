Buying Process
You can check out our Coming Soon page for when we expect upcoming litters. We will announce on our Facebook Page when we will start taking deposits on those litters. When litters are confirmed we ask for a deposit via PayPal / Venmo. Then once your pick is made, a second deposit is required.

Pictures and videos will be posted often on social media and on our website. We also create your own page to display your puppy. We try to post every 2 weeks as they continue to grow. Your puppy will receive de-worming medicine at 3 weeks and 6 weeks. At 6 weeks they will also get their first full round of shots. It will be vet checked and cleared before going to it's forever home. Once the vet gives the puppy his final stamp of approval, the remaining balance will be due before or at the time the puppy is picked up and you'll receive all vet documents at that time.
