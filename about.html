About the Goldendoodle

Goldendoodle Overview

Goldendoodles are a popular doodle breed known for their friendly, easy-going temperaments and soft, often low-shedding coats. They typically blend the loyalty and family focus of the Golden Retriever with the intelligence and trainability of the Poodle.

Bluegrass Doodles has experience raising Goldendoodle puppies in Kentucky, and many of those dogs now live with families all across the United States. You can browse pictures on our Goldendoodle litters page and review our buying process to understand how reservations work for our program. If you love the doodle look and temperament, you may also be interested in our Cockapoo puppies and Bernedoodle puppies.
