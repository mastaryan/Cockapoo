Our History

Bluegrass Doodle's began as an idea. My wife and I have 4 growing boys who longed for a puppy of their own. One Christmas we decided to make that dream a reality. When choosing a puppy breed we knew we had a few requests. We knew we wanted a dog that was small in size, hypoallergenic and that was great with children. Enjoying our new puppy we decide to share our precious Doodle's with others and started Bluegrass Doodles. Our goal is to bring joy and happiness to other families while providing forever homes for our precious puppies. Please take the time to explore our website and email or text me with any questions or concerns. The health and happiness of our pups is our number 1 priority. We can promise that each puppy will be home raised and children oriented.
