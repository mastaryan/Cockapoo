Our History

Bluegrass Doodle's began as an idea. My wife and I have 4 growing boys who longed for a puppy of their own. Last Christmas we decided to make that dream a reality. When choosing a puppy breed we knew we had a few requests. We knew we wanted a dog that was small in size, hypoallergenic and that was great with children. After a ton of research, we decided a Cockapoo was for us. This breed was hands down the best decision for our family. Now a year later we have made a family decision to begin our journey as cockapoo breeder’s. Our goal is to bring joy and happiness to other families while providing forever homes for our precious puppies. Please take the time to explore our website and email or text me with any questions or concerns. The health and happiness of our pups is our number 1 priority. We can promise that each puppy will be home raised and children oriented.
