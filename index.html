Our History

Located in the beautiful bluegrass state of Kentucky, our family has years of experience raising Cockapoos, Goldendoodles, Labradoodles, and Bernedoodles. Most importantly, we pride ourselves in raising happy, and healthy puppies. Our goal is to provide your family with the same joy and happiness each precious puppy brings to us. Please take the time to explore our website, Facebook, or Instagram and feel free to message me with any questions. From our family to yours...
