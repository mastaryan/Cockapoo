How the Deposit Works

• A $50 non-refundable deposit reserves your spot for a current or upcoming litter.
• Your deposit can be moved to a future litter if timing changes for your family.
• The deposit is applied toward your final puppy price at pickup.

After your payment is received, we will:

• Confirm your reservation and litter details.
• Share expected timelines and how selection will work.
• Keep you updated with photos and videos as puppies grow.

Remaining balance: Due at or before pickup (cash preferred). We can also send a Stripe link ahead of time or accept Venmo (Friends & Family) at pickup.

Puppies are typically ready to go home around 8 weeks of age after de-worming at 3 and 6 weeks, a 6-week vet check, and first shots. Each puppy goes home with a veterinary health record and our one-year genetic health guarantee. Families agree to continue regular veterinary care and keep vaccinations current for the guarantee to remain valid.
