What Happens Next

Ready-now pups: In many cases, we can confirm pickup directly in Messenger (without a deposit) and finalize payment at pickup.

If a deposit is needed, we’ll tell you which option to use:

• Stripe: Use the button on this page for a quick payment.
• Venmo: Use the Venmo link/QR code on this page.

After any payment is received (if applicable), we will:

• Confirm your puppy and pickup details.
• Share any last-minute info (food, schedule, etc.).
• Make sure you’re set up for a smooth transition home.

Final balance: Due at pickup (cash preferred). We can also accept Venmo Friends (not Goods & Services) at pickup.

Each puppy goes home with their health records and our one-year genetic health guarantee. Families agree to continue regular veterinary care and keep vaccinations current for the guarantee to remain valid.
